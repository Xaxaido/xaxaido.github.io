Privacy Notice Training Diary

Last updated: September 2, 2025

Overview

Training Diary respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It runs locally on your device and uses system permissions only to provide core functionality such as reminders.

Data Collection & Sharing

  • No personal data collection: Training Diary does not gather names, emails, health data, location, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No analytics or ads: The app does not include advertising SDKs or analytics tracking.
  • No third-party sharing: No data is transmitted to the developer or third parties.

Result: No user profiles, tracking, or cross-app data sharing.

Permissions Used & Why

Training Diary requests only the permissions required for timely training reminders.

android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Purpose: To send you notifications about upcoming training sessions that you schedule in the app.

Notifications can be controlled or disabled at any time in your system settings.

android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Purpose: To automatically restore your reminder alarms after the device restarts, ensuring your scheduled training alerts continue to work.

No personal data is accessed or transmitted during device boot; the app only re-schedules previously set alarms.

android.permission.SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM

Purpose: To schedule precise reminders for your upcoming training sessions so alerts fire at the exact time you expect.

Exact alarms are used strictly for user-initiated reminders.

Children’s Privacy

Training Diary is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Security

Training Diary stores user data such as name, date of birth, gender, and training records locally on the device in a private database.

These records never leave your device and are not transmitted to the developer or any third parties.

Access to this information is protected by the security features of the Android operating system. Users can manage the app’s permissions and data directly in system settings at any time.

Changes to This Notice

If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy or permissions, this notice will be updated accordingly.
